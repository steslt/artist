«Where the Magenta Horses Fall»

Acrylic 130×130

We seem to capture a frame...
It’s like turning around and freezing in a single moment — to truly see and appreciate how breathtaking the feelings, people, animals, plants, fruits, and all life around us are. How many energies flow through us, and how close, how present they all are, right here, right now. I am speaking about the Depth that exists even in the scent and color of a pear, in the beauty of a fish, in insects, in every living being. We can experience emotions just as strong through sound, color, and form as we do through love or drama.

I am speaking about the same force of motion, repetition, death, and birth that permeates our bodies. It is like an invisible power of growth within a tree — it sets the sap in motion... and ripens the fruit. It is this same force that creates love between us — attracts people toward one another, so that we give life to new beings, continuing the eternal, powerful movement of energies.
